When clueless and young Max moves from the Midwest to the Big Apple, he finds his new best friend by chance – a mutt, also named Max. The series follows the adventures of the new pair learning from each other’s individual needs.

Created by Lauren Ciaravalli, Directed by Kristen Harding and Marissa Rutka, Produced by Jean Claude Billmaier, Production Manager: Nick Krassowski, Director of Photography: Benjamin Dewey

Status / Release Date: Post Production – August 2013

Client: Pet Parents TV
