Acceptance

Visual Effects by Marabigo

"Acceptance" is a Singaporean Independent Film based on a true story about a scholar from India who lies about getting into Harvard and finds himself addicted to the shiny fruits of his "success.

Director: Ryan Chan
Screenplay by: Vishnu Hari and Ryan Chan
Marabigo: VFX- Roto, Screen Replacements, Motion Graphics and Clean-Up
